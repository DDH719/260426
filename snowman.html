SNOWMAN

Portable Lighting Collection

Overview

SNOWMAN8 Portable is a compact lighting object designed to bring warm illumination into everyday spaces. Inspired by the iconic Snowman series, the portable version combines playful form with practical mobility.

The magnetic base and included accessories allow the lamp to be attached, hung, carried and displayed in various ways, transforming light into an interactive experience.

Specifications

  • Light Source : Integrated LED (2700K / 5000K)
  • Battery : 3.7V 900mAh
  • Size : 80 × 80 × 95 mm
  • Material : PC + PET + ABS + Silicon + Magnet
  • Cable : USB-C 30cm
  • Water Resistance : IP44
